How to make wontons

Description

This is the perfect recipe for making wontons. They go really well with soup but can be paired with anything.

Ingredients

  • circle wonton wrappers
  • water
  • pot
  • a pair of gloves
  • a bowl to put your filling
  • any meat of your choosing
  • any vegetables of your choosing

Directions

  1. Clean your hands and wear your gloves. We will be working with raw meat so the gloves are very important.
  2. Take out a bowl for your filling and any ingredients you want to pair together in your wontons. Remember to marinate you meat (I usually use soy sauce, oyster sauce, and salt).
  3. Place them in your bowl and and use your hands to mix well.
  4. When all your ingrdients are evenly mixed together, open you package of circle wonton wrappings.
  5. Using your hands, take a small pinch of your filling and place it in the middle of your wonton wrapping.
  6. Take a little water and spread it on the edges of the wonton wrapping.
  7. Carefully, close your wrapping around your filling. It does not matter how it looks.
  8. Take a pot and boil water in it. After the water boils, add the wontons. Once the wontons are floating up, cook for another minute. Take the wontons out and you're done!!
